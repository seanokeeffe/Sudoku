Sudoku

Welcome to Sudoku!

The rules are simple: Fill up the grid so that the numbers 1 through 9 appear once in every row, column, and 3x3 block!

To play this game, first select on a number, 1 through 9, on the bottom number pad.

When the number you want is highlighted, press on the cell you want to put that number in

Good Luck!

Enjoy!
